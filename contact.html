Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. Simple websites take 2-4 weeks, while complex e-commerce platforms can take 2-4 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you work with clients outside Africa?

Absolutely! While we specialize in African markets, we work with clients globally. Our remote-first approach allows us to collaborate effectively across time zones.

What's included in your development services?

Our services include planning, design, development, testing, deployment, and 3 months of free support. We also provide training and documentation for your team.

Can you help improve an existing website?

Yes! We offer website audits, performance optimization, feature additions, and complete redesigns. We can work with your existing codebase or recommend a fresh start.
